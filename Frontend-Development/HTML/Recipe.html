Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe

Indulge your taste buds with the irresistible aroma and delectable taste of homemade chocolate chip cookies. Whether you're a seasoned baker or a novice in the kitchen, this classic recipe will guide you step by step to create the perfect batch of warm, gooey, and oh-so-satisfying treats. Loaded with rich, melty chocolate chips nestled within a buttery, chewy dough, these cookies are sure to become a beloved staple in your baking repertoire.
So, grab your apron, preheat the oven, and get ready to experience the pure joy of baking and savoring these mouthwatering chocolate chip cookies straight from your own oven.

Prep Time: 15 minutes

Cook Time: 12 minutes

Total Time: 27 minutes

Go to instructions

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Unsalted butter, softened 1 cup (2 sticks)
Granulated sugar 3/4 cup
Packed brown sugar 3/4 cup
Vanilla extract 1 teaspoon
Large eggs 2
All-purpose flour 2 1/4 cups
Baking soda 1 teaspoon
Salt 1/2 teaspoon
Semisweet chocolate chips 2 cups

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, beat the softened butter, granulated sugar, brown sugar, and vanilla extract until creamy.
  3. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition.
  4. In a separate bowl, combine the flour, baking soda, and salt. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the butter mixture, mixing well.
  5. Stir in the chocolate chips until they are evenly distributed in the cookie dough.
  6. Drop rounded tablespoons of dough onto the prepared baking sheet, spacing them about 2 inches apart.
  7. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until the edges are golden brown. The centers may appear slightly undercooked, but they will set as the cookies cool.
  8. Remove the cookies from the oven and let them cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes. Then transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.
